About

HTS Consulting is a company from the Netherlands. We supply products and services in the field of animal production. The main field of interest is poultry production. Our focus are emerging and developing markets mainly in Eastern Europe and Africa. We want to be a 1-stop-shop for our customers. They can procure their equipment and commodities through us if possible. Our back ground is the poultry industry in the Netherlands.
